Organisation results

An overview of Civil Service People Survey results by organisation

On this page

  • All organisations
  • Main departments

Each year around 100 government departments, agencies and other UK Civil Service organisations take part in the People Survey each year1. This page provides a summary of all organisation scores as well as a focus on the results of the main departments, use the drop-down below to view the results for a particular organisation.

All organisations

Across all most all questions and measures in the People Survey there is substantial variation in organisation scores. In 2024 the range of organisation scores for the headline engagement index ranged from 52% to 80.5%.
